About Trailblazers

Celebrated as the most significant corporate forum in the heart of Sri Lanka, Trailblazers is an exemplary movement initiated by AIESEC in the University of Peradeniya that brings together young generations and leads them to step into the future, overcoming the challenges ahead while filling the gap between the corporate world and the youth. This is a legacy event of AIESEC in University of Peradeniya with an exciting history since 2017.
